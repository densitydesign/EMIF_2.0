## Compact Field Guide

1. **Priority**: Critical (🔴), High (🟠), Medium (🟡), Low (🟢)
2. **Environment**: Combine OS + Browser/Device
3. **Title**: Keep under 60 chars with key details
4. Use `@mentions` for assignees/reporters
